== Description ==

 WP Fortune Wheel interesting way to get subscribers of your WordPress website. WP Fortune Wheel offers visitors to fill in their email addresses and spins to get prizes. This is the best way to collect email from visitors on your site, they will be pleased to fill in their email address. The prize will be sent to visitors email automatically.
